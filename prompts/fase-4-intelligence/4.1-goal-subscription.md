## Prompt

```
Baca context: AGENTS.md, database-schema.md (goals, subscriptions), business-rules.md (Subscription Warning), api-conventions.md.

═══ 1. GOAL TRACKING ═══

Backend:
- CRUD goals (sesuai schema)
- Auto-link: goal type='emergency_fund' → auto-track current_amount dari EF accounts
- Auto-link: goal type='debt_payoff' → track dari debt outstanding progress
- Progress: current_amount / target_amount × 100%
- Projected completion date: berdasarkan average monthly contribution rate
- POST /api/v1/goals/:id/contribute → record contribution (buat transfer ke linked account)

Frontend: GoalsPage.tsx (/goals)
- Card grid: per goal with circular progress indicator
- Goal types: 🛡️ Dana Darurat, 💳 Pelunasan Utang, 🏠 DP Rumah, ✈️ Liburan, 📚 Pendidikan, 🎯 Custom
- Card: icon, name, progress ring, "Rp 15jt / Rp 30jt", target date, projected completion
- Detail: milestone timeline, contribution history, monthly contribution trend chart
- "Kontribusi" button → quick transfer

═══ 2. SUBSCRIPTION TRACKER ═══

Backend:
- CRUD subscriptions (sesuai schema)
- Warning detection (dari business-rules.md): last_used_date > 60 hari → warning
- GET /api/v1/subscriptions/summary → total monthly cost, active count, warning list

Frontend: SubscriptionsPage.tsx (/subscriptions)
- List: name, provider, amount, next renewal, last used, status
- Total monthly cost card prominent
- Warning badges: "⚠️ Belum digunakan 90 hari — Rp 150rb/bulan"
- Reminder sebelum renewal
- Toggle active/inactive
```
